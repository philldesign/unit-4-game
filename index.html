Crystals Collector!

You will be given a ramdom number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystals you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to ramdom number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the ramdom number The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
